If you couldn't tell already, I love everything computers. I love development, and every aspect that is in it. Anyone, anywhere, and anytime. I also reflect on this motto because of how I work. This may be good or bad but, I work in short bursts. So because of that, it can be while driving somewhere or being at Starbucks, I might just get a feeling of motivation and enthusiasm to develop something, or continue any of any existing projects. This may or may not be linked due to the caffeine, but non-the-less, I would still continue doing the work!
